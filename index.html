Market Intelligence

Why Nicaragua, Why Now

The Emerald Coast is at an inflection point. Here's the data behind the opportunity you'll see firsthand.

8–12% Annual Appreciation

Coastal property prices are projected to grow 8–12% annually through 2029, with premium lots and beachfront homes leading the surge. Nicaragua's GDP growth of 3.5–4% and the 2025 Foreign Investment Law underpin this trajectory.

$400M Coastal Highway

A new highway connecting the Costa Rican border through San Juan del Sur and the entire Emerald Coast is rapidly being built, dramatically improving access and property values.

Full Foreign Ownership

Foreigners hold the same ownership rights as Nicaraguan citizens under the Constitution (Art. 44) and Foreign Investment Law (Ley 344). Clear titled land available — always verify title and work with a local attorney.

Record Tourism Growth

Nearly 2 million tourist arrivals in 2024, driving strong rental demand. Featured in Travel + Leisure (2025) and leading travel publications as an emerging surf and wellness destination.

Unmatched Value

Raw land from ~$25/m². Compared to Costa Rica's Guanacaste, the Emerald Coast offers similar coastline at a fraction of the price — but the gap is closing fast.

Gated Communities

Rancho Santana (2,700 acres) and Hacienda Iguana offer resort-grade amenities: golf, pools, security, and beachfront access with proven appreciation.

The Experience

More Than Business

Your investment trip isn't just meetings and property tours. It's a taste of the life that comes with it.

World-Class Surf

Consistent offshore winds 300+ days a year. Popoyo, Playa Colorado, Playa Santana — breaks for every level from beginner to barrel hunter.

Year-Round Summer

Daytime highs of 86–90°F year-round. Tropical dry season November–May. Perfect for the digital nomad or retiree seeking warm weather 365 days.

Low Cost of Living

Groceries, healthcare, and daily expenses at a fraction of US or Europe. A high quality of life on a modest budget.

Growing Expat Community

A welcoming international community of surfers, entrepreneurs, retirees, and remote workers from North America, Europe, and beyond.
